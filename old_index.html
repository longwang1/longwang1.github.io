    • About Me

    I am Long Wang (王龙), a tenured associated professor at Tsinghua University. I am leading the REliability And Security Of Networks and Systems (REASONS) lab. My research areas focus on resiliency, security and understanding of systems, services and networks, especially when they are complicated, intelligent, autonomous, dynamic and/or software-defined.
